Wearable Health Data (Oura Ring)

cruzam:score xsd:integer
Numeric score from source system (e.g., Oura sleep score, readiness score). 0-100 scale.
cruzam:day xsd:date
Calendar date the measurement covers.
cruzam:bedtimeStart xsd:dateTime
Start of sleep session as recorded by wearable device.
cruzam:bedtimeEnd xsd:dateTime
End of sleep session as recorded by wearable device.
cruzam:efficiency xsd:integer
Sleep efficiency percentage (time asleep / time in bed * 100).
cruzam:avgHeartRate xsd:decimal
Average heart rate during sleep session in beats per minute.
cruzam:avgHrv xsd:integer
Average heart rate variability during sleep in milliseconds.
cruzam:avgBreath xsd:decimal
Average breathing rate during sleep in breaths per minute.
cruzam:deepSleepDuration xsd:integer
Total deep sleep duration in seconds.
cruzam:awakeTime xsd:integer
Total time spent awake during sleep session in seconds.
cruzam:contributorDeepSleep xsd:integer
Oura sleep contributor score for deep sleep amount (0-100).
cruzam:contributorEfficiency xsd:integer
Oura sleep contributor score for sleep efficiency (0-100).
cruzam:contributorLatency xsd:integer
Oura sleep contributor score for sleep onset latency (0-100). Higher = fell asleep faster.
cruzam:contributorRemSleep xsd:integer
Oura sleep contributor score for REM sleep amount (0-100).
cruzam:contributorRestfulness xsd:integer
Oura sleep contributor score for restfulness / lack of disturbances (0-100).
cruzam:contributorTiming xsd:integer
Oura sleep contributor score for sleep timing alignment with circadian rhythm (0-100).
cruzam:contributorTotalSleep xsd:integer
Oura sleep contributor score for total sleep duration (0-100).
cruzam:contributorActivityBalance xsd:integer
Oura readiness contributor score for activity balance over recent days (0-100).
cruzam:contributorBodyTemperature xsd:integer
Oura readiness contributor score for body temperature deviation (0-100).
cruzam:contributorPreviousDayActivity xsd:integer
Oura readiness contributor score for previous day activity level (0-100).
cruzam:contributorPreviousNight xsd:integer
Oura readiness contributor score for previous night sleep quality (0-100).
cruzam:contributorRecoveryIndex xsd:integer
Oura readiness contributor score for resting heart rate recovery (0-100).
cruzam:contributorRestingHeartRate xsd:integer
Oura readiness contributor score for resting heart rate relative to baseline (0-100).

Insight Classification

cruzam:category xsd:string
Thematic category of an insight (e.g., 'sleep', 'activity', 'network').
cruzam:severity xsd:string
Severity level of an insight finding (e.g., 'low', 'medium', 'high', 'critical').
cruzam:pattern xsd:string
Machine-readable pattern identifier for an insight (e.g., 'short_sleep_fast_onset').

Social Network Analysis (LinkedIn)

cruzam:postAuthor xsd:string
LinkedIn public identifier (slug) of the original post author.
cruzam:reshareAuthor xsd:string
LinkedIn public identifier (slug) of the person who reshared the post.
cruzam:taggedPeople xsd:integer
Number of people tagged in a social media post.
cruzam:commentParticipants xsd:integer
Number of unique people who commented on a post.
cruzam:totalUniquePeople xsd:integer
Total number of unique people involved in a post (tagged + commenters, deduplicated).
cruzam:companiesMentioned xsd:integer
Number of distinct companies mentioned or tagged in a post.
cruzam:topic xsd:string
Primary topic or event identifier (e.g., 'PACT2026').
cruzam:taggedConnected xsd:integer
Number of tagged people who are 1st-degree connections.
cruzam:taggedTotal xsd:integer
Total number of tagged people in the analysis set.
cruzam:commentConnected xsd:integer
Number of comment participants who are 1st-degree connections.
cruzam:commentTotal xsd:integer
Total number of comment participants in the analysis set.
cruzam:totalConnected xsd:integer
Total number of people who are 1st-degree connections across all categories.
cruzam:totalPeople xsd:integer
Total number of people in the analysis set.
cruzam:overlapRate xsd:integer
Percentage of people who are 1st-degree connections (0-100).
cruzam:pichtrConnections xsd:integer
Number of 1st-degree connections at PICHTR (Pacific International Center for High Technology Research).
cruzam:defenceAuConnections xsd:integer
Number of 1st-degree connections in Australian Defence organizations.
